Meet Your Guide

Hi, I'm Kaia.

I have been a flight attendant for over eight years, flying hundreds of routes across Europe, Asia, and the Americas. Every single day I watch passengers board with white knuckles and shallow breath.

Before I joined the crew, I was terrified of flying myself. What changed everything was understanding exactly what happens in your body during anxiety — and learning that fear, no matter how overwhelming, can be navigated.

"I have held the hands of hundreds of nervous flyers at 35,000 feet. This workbook is what I wish I could give every single one of them."
